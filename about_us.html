About us

Since our inception in 2010, we have been committed to providing the highest standard of services through our laundry and dry cleaning services. We mainly give attention to working efficiently while keeping proper communication with our clients and customers.


We focus to ensure excellence through improvement. Our laundry services cover a wide range including dry cleaning, steaming and pressing, premium wash, sofa cleaning, polishing, carpet cleaning, and live laundry. We inculcate free pickup and delivery of your clothes at the doorstep along with the advanced mechanised process to bring the best out of our laundry services.
